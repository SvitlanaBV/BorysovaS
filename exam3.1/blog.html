Reimer Wedding Footage

Date: February 14, 2020 Author:

Svetlana Borisova

It should come as no surprise: High-touch cinematography is just as important to your wedding day as expert photography. Wedding coverage no longer begs the question of whether you'll have a videographer, but rather, which talented video team you'll collaborate with.

The best videographers collaborate seamlessly with your photographer, planner, designer, stylist, and other members of your creative team. They understand when and where to utilize drone footage, edit your wedding with music and a style that speaks to your event's look and feel, and ensure the film feels cohesive and consistent with the look of your photographs. True pros understand that photography and cinematography aren't two distinct teams on the wedding day—despite their (most likely) being two separate businesses.
